error recognition handguide

-led on
	-> no disk detected

-led blinks fast 3 times
	-> disk detected but no disk color detected

-led on and a fast sirene goes on
	-> sensors blocked

-led blinks slow 5 times and fast sirene goes on
	-> pusher is stuck

-led blinks fast 5 times and fast sirene goes on
	->grabber is stuck

-led blinks very fast 10 times and slow sirene goes on
	-> the buffer zone is full

-led of sirene of
	-> no error
